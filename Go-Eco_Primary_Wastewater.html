Primary Treatment Vermicomposting Digester    Secondary Treatment Vermifilter

Constructed twin chamber primary vermifilter (vermicomposting digester).
This is fed by a flush toilet in the house. 
Twin chambers offer hygenic and simple removal of composted waste after a resting period (usually 5 years).
Primary treated effluent is either disposed of in underground soakage trenches or alternatively surface
irrigated after undergoing secondary treatment.
