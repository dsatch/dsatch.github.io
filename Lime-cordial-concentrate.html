Lime cordial concentrate


Squeeze as many limes as you like. Add the zest from one in twenty limes.

Weight the juice and add an equal weight of sugar. Add 1 teaspoon citric acid for every kilogram of sugar.

Warm it gently on the stove, stirring constantly. It only needs to get warm, enough heat only to dissolve the sugar. Leave it for an hour or two before straining into bottles. You might need to put some in the freezer if you want it to last for ages.
