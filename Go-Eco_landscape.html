I design and implement natural landscapes that fit in with Northlands lush surroundings of vegetation and bush. 
Creative landscaping involves combining aesthetic appeal with practical considerations like shelter,
sunlight/shade, microclimate and vistas.
An understanding of each plant's climatic and soil requirements and how it interacts with surrounding
vegetation is essential to providing a lasting, useful and attractive landscape.
