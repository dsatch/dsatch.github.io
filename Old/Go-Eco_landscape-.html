I design and implement natural landscapes that fit in with Northlands lush surroundings of vegetation and bush. Have a look at some landscapes I have created here in Northland.

Creative landscaping involves combining aesthetic appeal with practical considerations like shelter, sunlight/shade, microclimate and vistas.

An understanding of each plant's climatic requirements and how it interacts with surrounding vegetation is essential to providing a lasting, useful and attractive landscape.

My landscapes are inexpensive and I provide a no-cost and no-obligation site visit to discuss your requirements.
